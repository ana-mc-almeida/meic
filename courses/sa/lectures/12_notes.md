## Layered Style

Technological Layer means that the layers are not related to the business logic, but to the technology used aka the same layers to diffenrent projects

Layers: (technological layer)
1. Presentation
2. Service
3. Domain
4. Data Access


To communicate with an higher layer, uses **callbacks**.
