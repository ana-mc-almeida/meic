## Event-Based Styles

Exists an variety of event-based styles.

### Publish-Subscribe Style

Implicit invocation is when the sender does not know the identity of the receiver. The sender only knows the event and the receiver is responsible for subscribing to the event. This is the basis of the publish-subscribe style.
