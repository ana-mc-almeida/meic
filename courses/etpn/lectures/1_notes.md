## Processes in Many Organizations

1. Lead-to-Quote
   - Lead = potential customer
   - Quote = price for a product or service
   - Process of converting a lead to a quote
2. Quote-to-Order
   - The quote is avaluated by the customer
   - Some negotiation may be required
   - After a quote is accepted, it is converted to an order
3. Order-to-Cash
    - Process of delivering the product or service to the customer
4. Procure-to-Pay
5. Application-to-Aproval
6. Fault-to-Resolution

## Elements of a Business Process

1. **Events**: Something that happens in the business process
   - Have no duration
   - Cannot be associated with effort or impact
2. **Activities**: Work that is performed as part of the business process
   - Have duration
   - Can be associated with effort and resources to execute and produce something
   - If the activity is simple and can be done in one step, it is called a task
3. **Decision Points (or gateways)**: Decide the next step in the process
   - Analyze the data from the previous steps
